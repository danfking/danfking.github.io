## Resume Landscape

Interactive 3D portfolio where you drive a Land Cruiser through a landscape of my work history. Each landmark is a job or project you can pull up to and inspect.

`Three.js` `Jolt Physics` `JavaScript`

[GitHub](https://github.com/danfking/side-projects/tree/main/resume-landscape) · [Live Demo](https://danfking.github.io/side-projects/resume-landscape/)

## Abyssal Oracle

AI creature generator that evolves pixel art sprites using a neural network, then extrudes them into 3D models. The viewer shows creatures in a reef-like environment.

[GitHub](https://github.com/danfking/side-projects/tree/main/abyssal-oracle)

## ASCII Align

CLI tool that fixes misaligned ASCII art from LLMs. Detects box-drawing characters and table structures, then realigns them so the columns actually line up.

`TypeScript` `Node.js` `npm`

[GitHub](https://github.com/danfking/side-projects/tree/main/ascii-align)
