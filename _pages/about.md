I'm Dan King, a software engineer based in the US. I build games, tools, and occasionally strange AI creatures.

Most of my side projects live in a single monorepo and get built with heavy use of Claude Code as a development partner. I'm interested in what becomes possible when you pair an experienced developer with AI tooling that actually fits into a real workflow.

During the day I work on enterprise software. Evenings and weekends I spend on [the projects listed here](/projects/), which range from a minesweeper roguelike targeting Steam to a CLI tool for fixing broken ASCII art.

You can find me on [GitHub](https://github.com/danfking).
